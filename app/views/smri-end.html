What is a ‘person with SMRI’?
A person with Significant Managerial Responsibility or Influence (SMRI) is someone who:
  • makes key decisions about how a heat network is run
  • holds a senior position, such as a director, board member, or partner
  • influences company policy, operations, or financial management
What are the fit and proper criteria?
The criteria assess whether an individual is suitable based on integrity, competence, and experience.

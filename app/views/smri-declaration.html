Significant managerial responsibility or influence (SMRI) declaration

You must declare whether everybody with SMRI at Radienteco Ltd is fit and proper, in order for anybody at the organisation to send us heat network information.

What is a 'person with SMRI'?

A person has SMRI if they play a role in either:

  • making decisions about how the whole or a substantial part of the organisation is managed or organised
  • actually managing or organising the whole or a substantial part of the organisation’s activities

For example, this includes chief officers, directors, and senior managers.

What you need to do

  1. Identify everyone in your organisation with SMRI.
  2. Ask each of them to confirm whether they meet the fit and proper criteria.
  3. Return to this service and submit your SMRI declaration.
  4. If someone does not meet the criteria, we’ll contact you for further information.

You must also confirm that your organisation:

  • has a process in place to ensure all SMRI individuals are fit and proper
  • regularly reassesses whether they remain fit and proper
What are the fit and proper criteria?
  • No unspent criminal convictions
  • Sound financial standing
  • Relevant qualifications and experience

Read the full criteria

How we’ll review your SMRI declaration

When you submit heat network data, we’ll use the most recent SMRI declaration we have.

If your declaration is more than 3 months old, it will be marked as ‘expired’, and you’ll need to submit a new one.
